Our Services

What we are best at.

Mowing, trimming, edging, blowing

Our regular lawncare maintenance includes mowing, trimming, edging, and blowing. We pay extra attention to detail to ensure your yard is just right.

Mulch & Rock Installation

We provide mulch and rock installation that is done right and comes with a weed barrier. Mulch and rock will improve the overall look of your yard. Mulch comes in black, red, chocolate, or natural and rock comes in a wide variety of colors and sizes.

Aeration

Aerating your lawn relieves soil compaction and enhances grass growth. Any lawn can benefit from aeration when it's timed well and done properly.

Fertilization

Fertilization will help your lawn by improving overall health and making it greener, thicker, and lusher. It will not only help your lawn look better but it will also last longer.

Weed control

We can get rid of the most common and most stubborn weeds in your lawn. Weed control is key to overall health of your lawn and making your yard stand out from the rest.

Landscape Design

Whether you know exactly what you want or don't have a clue...we can help. We can make your vision come to life or give recommendations on what will make your yard look it's best.

FAQs

Your questions answered

Are you insured?

Yes. I am fully insured and carry a 1,000,000 policy for liability, casualty, and property damage.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes! We will be glad to come out and take a look at your yard and give you a free quote and there is no obligation!

How do you schedule your clients?

I do my best to work setup the maintenance schedule according to what is best for each client. If the property has a fence, the client doesn't have to be home for me to service the lawn. Payment is not needed until the client inspects the work to make sure their expectations are met. If the client lets me know ahead of time I will also make sure any special requests are taken care of.
